# Cloud Risk Assessment

## Purpose

Use this template to assess risk across cloud environments before approving major changes or remediation plans.

## Risk Areas

- security risk
- compliance risk
- operational risk
- architectural risk
- cost risk
- resilience risk

## Assessment View

| Area | Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Security | High | Access gaps | Improve identity baseline |
| Compliance | Medium | Evidence gaps | Map controls to evidence |
| Operations | High | Unclear ownership | Define service owner |
| Architecture | Medium | Inconsistent patterns | Enforce standards |
| Cost | Medium | Allocation gaps | Improve tagging |
| Resilience | High | Recovery uncertainty | Test failover paths |

## Decision

- Accept risk
- Mitigate risk
- Escalate risk
- Reject change
